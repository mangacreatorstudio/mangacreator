Process

How It Works

Three simple steps — from idea to manga in under a minute.

1

Write Your Prompt

Describe your story in any language. A few sentences is enough — hero, setting, conflict. No experience needed.

2

Choose Your Style

Pick a genre and art style — Shonen, Shojo, Seinen, or Josei. Customize tone, pacing, and panel count.

3

Get Your Manga

AI generates your full chapter with panels, captions, and dialogue. Download as PDF, PNG, or continue the story.

About Us

We Believe Every Story Deserves to be Seen

MangaCreator was born from a simple frustration — too many incredible stories never get told because their creators can't draw. We built an AI that bridges that gap.

Our team of manga artists, writers, and engineers spent two years training a model that understands not just images, but story structure, pacing, tension, and emotion — everything that makes manga come alive.

Today, creators in over 40 countries use MangaCreator to publish original stories, pitch ideas, and bring their imagination to the page.
